EES RobotJan - May 2018

During College, I coded and helped build a self navigating robot for the Engineering Education Scheme (EES), using an Arduino for the processing unit and a ultrasound censor for collision detection. The body was made using two sheets of acrylic and a Lego frame to support the motors and track frames.
